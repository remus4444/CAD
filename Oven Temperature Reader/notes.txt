Operation concept:

1. Flip a switch to turn the device on
2. Use up and down buttons to adjust the target temperature
3. Hit a central button to confirm and start monitoring
4. A buzzer rings when the target temperature is hit
5. The user pressed the confirm button to turn the buzzer off

Required parts:

1. A housing (3d printed)
2. A microcontroller (Arduino nano)
3. Three buttons (RP3502ARED)
4. One switch (ANT11SECQE)
5. A power source (9V battery + snap connector)
6. A display (SSD1306 0.96 inch I2C OLED)
7. A buzzer (PS1240P02BT)
